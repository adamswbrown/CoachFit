# Getting Started with CoachFit

Welcome to CoachFit! This guide will help you get started with the platform quickly.

---

## Account Types

CoachFit supports three types of accounts:

1. **Client Accounts** - For individuals tracking their fitness progress
2. **Coach Accounts** - For fitness professionals managing client cohorts
3. **Admin Accounts** - For platform administrators managing users and system settings

---

## Creating Your Account

### Sign Up Options

You can sign up using:

- **Google OAuth** (recommended) - One-click sign in with your Google account
- **Email and Password** - Traditional sign in with email and password

### First Time Setup

1. Navigate to [https://your-coachfit-url.com](https://your-coachfit-url.com)
2. Click "Sign Up" if you don't have an account
3. Choose your authentication method:
   - **Google**: Click "Sign in with Google" and authorize
   - **Email/Password**: Enter your email and create a password
4. Complete the onboarding process based on your role
5. Start using CoachFit!

## Quick Start by Role

### For Clients

1. **Sign up** using Google or email/password
2. **Wait for coach assignment** - Your coach will invite you to a cohort
3. **Start logging check-ins** - Log your weight, steps, calories, and sleep quality
4. **View your progress** - See your trends and history over time

[Read the complete Client Guide →](./clients.md)

### For Coaches

1. **Sign up** or get invited by an admin
2. **Create your first cohort** - Organize your clients into groups
3. **Invite clients** - Send invitations via email
4. **Review analytics** - Track client progress and provide guidance

[Read the complete Coach Guide →](./coaches.md)

### For Admins

1. **Get admin role** - Assigned by existing admin
2. **Manage users** - Add/remove roles, reset passwords
3. **Create coaches** - Invite new coaches to the platform
4. **Monitor system** - View platform-wide statistics

[Read the complete Admin Guide →](./admins.md)

## Authentication

### Google OAuth (Recommended)

The easiest way to sign in:

1. Click "Sign in with Google"
2. Choose your Google account
3. Authorize CoachFit
4. You're logged in!

**Benefits**:
- ✅ One-click sign in
- ✅ No password to remember
- ✅ Secure Google authentication

### Email and Password

Traditional authentication:

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Sign In"

**Password Requirements**:
- At least 8 characters
- Combination of letters and numbers recommended

### Switching Between Devices

Your account is accessible from any device:
- Desktop computer
- Laptop
- Tablet
- Mobile phone

Just sign in with the same credentials on any device.

## First Steps After Signing Up

### As a Client

1. **Complete onboarding** - Learn how CoachFit works
2. **Wait for coach invitation** - Your coach will add you to a cohort
3. **Explore your dashboard** - Familiarize yourself with the interface
4. **Log your first check-in** - Start tracking your fitness data

### As a Coach

1. **Complete onboarding** - Understand the coach workflow
2. **Create your first cohort** - Name it based on your program
3. **Invite your first clients** - Add client email addresses
4. **Review the dashboard** - Explore the analytics and client management tools

### As an Admin

1. **Review user list** - See all users in the system
2. **Check cohort assignments** - Ensure coaches have access
3. **Create coach accounts** - Invite new coaches if needed
4. **Explore admin tools** - Familiarize yourself with management features
